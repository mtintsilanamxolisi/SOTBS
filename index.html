Unlocking Potential, One Lesson at a Time

School of the Black Sheep is a premier tutoring service provider established in 2021, offering personalized door-to-door and online tutoring for all grades, as well as specialized assistance for university students in Mathematics, Science, and Accounting. Our mission is to provide high-quality, accessible education to students, fostering academic excellence and lifelong learning.

Why Choose Us?

At School of the Black Sheep we believe in the ability, particularly the learning ability of every child and general human being. We believe there are many life obstacles that may deter and or distort one from their set goals and achievements but anything in a book can be learnt with proper teaching, determination, hard work and patience. We want to help the children of South Africa see Math as a subject that they can enjoy, after all “Math is Fun”.

Mission & Vision

Our mission and vision is to become a leading educational support service in South Africa, recognized for our commitment to student success and educational innovation. We aim to empower students with the knowledge and skills necessary to excel academically through personalized and innovative tutoring services.
